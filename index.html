Profile

  • Yuyang Dong, Man.
  • Born in Beijing, China. 1991
  • Received the B.S. degree from China University of Mining & Technology, Beijing, 2013.
  • Received the M.S. and Ph.D. degree from University of Tsukuba, Japan, 2014-2019.
  • Now, a principal researcher in NEC Generative AI Center and Data Science Research Lab, Japan.

Research Field

  • Spatial index and vector search (PhD)
  • LLM/ML/NLP for DB, DB for LLM/ML/NLP
  • LLM/VLM for tabular data

News!!!

  • We released Jellyfish-7B, 8B, 13B on huggingface -- our large language model designed for data preprocessing. With small parameters, Jellyfish allows for cost-effective local execution without compromising data security and delivers performance on a par with GPT-4 on many data preprocessing tasks such as entity matching, data imputation, and error detection.
